EECS 498 Project 1: CSE Simulator

Fall 2023

Completed with Nick Antony

Description

A virtual reality simulation of room 1695 in the Bob and Betty Beyster Building at the University of Michigan. The room is a computer lab currently closed due to construction, but the simulation offers a semi-realistic interpretation for new EECS students who have yet to experience the joys of staying up until 2 AM working on a project.

Features

  • Desks, computers, and dividers
  • Faint office-like background ambience
  • Togglable lights
  • Litter that can be picked up and disposed of
  • Copy machine that can duplicate trash
  • Working fire alarm (totally legal to pull, unlike real life)
  • Various NPC students to talk to
  • Interactable whiteboard to take notes on
  • Mark Zuckerberg running around the room looking for students to recruit
  • Day-night cycle to simulate spending too long working on EECS projects
  • Unlockable sticker box full of highly sought-after U-M EECS stickers
