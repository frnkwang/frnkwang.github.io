EECS 498 Project 3: DNA VR

Fall 2023

Completed with Nick Antony, Chenyi Lin, Sohavni Singh, Jamie Zhou

Description

A VR game to teach DNA transcription and translation to high schoolers. Swim around inside a cell, build mRNA, create proteins, and defend against viruses.
Project website

Features

  • Swim around a model of the inside of a cell, complete with various organelles
  • Collect nucleotides and attach them to DNA to transcribe into mRNA
  • Attach mRNA to ribosomes to start translation
  • Find tRNAs with matching anticodons to form proteins
  • Package proteins and deliver them out of the cell
  • Defend against viruses using lysosomes
  • Track your efficiency with a leaderboard system
