EECS 498 Project 2: A2 Go

Fall 2023

Completed with Nick Antony

Description

An augmented reality game similar to Pokemon Go, set in Ann Arbor. Explore the city of Ann Arbor to find landmarks, unlock seeds, and plant trees.

Features

  • Walk around AA to find landmarks at important locations to unlock seeds
  • Pan around an interactive map to search for landmarks
  • Purchase seeds from the shop and plant them in augmented reality
  • Watch trees grow over time and start generating revenue as tourism increases
  • Witness Ann Arbor's infamously fat squirrels as they scurry around your trees in AR
  • Appease the squirrels by throwing them acorns
  • Protect your trees from squirrels by placing fences
